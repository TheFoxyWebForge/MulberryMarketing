About Us



Mulberry Studio is a creative agency. If you are looking for high-quality work with a team of highly creative, innovative & tech-savvy people you’ve come to the right place. We have a customer-centric approach and provide our services efficiently and professionally. Be it designing a logo or website or gaining organic followers on social media platforms our team at Mulberry Studio assures that all these tasks are done in a creative & efficient way. We provide premium quality services. It is the one-stop solution for all your design-related requirements. We provide different services under one roof!




At Mulberry Studio it’s majorly about one thing: Working with our clients to deliver the best possible design so that the story of the product is communicated in an effective way to the target audience. With a culture of collaboration & a highly creative team, we are interested in what’s next to provide our clients with. We love what we do & we wouldn’t have it any other way. Every project has its own unique demands, our extended team of strategists, creatives, and technology specialists are always eager to pitch in so that every demand of the client is taken care of.

HOW DO WE DO IT?

The first and foremost thing to do is to plan. We take into consideration the client’s advice and plan the designs according to their needs.

After our team gets the brief from the clients then the creative juices flow by our talented designers. Designs are made by keeping in mind the tone of the brand. The vision and objectives of a brand are taken into consideration while designing.

The creative team writes the content to help the client’s business get more conversions. Writing innovative and creative content is necessary for any brand to get people intrigued and take action.


PROMOTING & ANALYSING

With the help of organic marketing the promotion is done and the designing, content, and promotion are taken into consideration to track the results.
